⚖️ The Five Laws of Safe Agency

I. Epistemic Conservation ▼
Uncertainty cannot be destroyed — only narrowed by evidence. Every `infer` returns an `Uncertain` with a probability interval [lo, hi]. U1‑U6 axioms enforced by compiler and VM.
II. Capillary Authority ▼
Authority flows only through unforgeable, attenuable tokens. The trust of a composed system is the lattice meet of its components. Conjunction safety checked via hypergraph closure.
III. Homeostatic Control ▼
Five lexicographic corrigibility heads (deference, switch preservation, truthfulness, low impact, task reward). A control meter and dead‑man's switch enforce safe‑park when principal contact is lost.
IV. Causal Accountability ▼
Every state transition is recorded in a Merkle‑proofed provenance DAG. Deterministic replay enables the do‑calculus and counterfactual surgery.
V. Stratified Expressivity ▼
Grammar is partitioned into S0 ⊂ S1 ⊂ S2 ⊂ S3. S0 is LLM‑generation‑friendly (~50 productions); S3 is the kernel. Escalation requires human sign‑off and adversarial simulation.
